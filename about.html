About Taniti

History

Taniti was first discovered in 1602 by Portuguese explorers. It has since become known for its lush beaches and rich cultural heritage.

Attractions

  • Pristine white-sand beaches
  • Guided snorkeling tours
  • Historic cultural museums

Transportation

From the cruise port, visitors can choose:

  • Water Taxi ($10 per person)
  • Shuttle Bus ($5 per person)
  • Ferry Service ($8 per person)

Visitor Information

The legal drinking age in Taniti is 18 years old.
